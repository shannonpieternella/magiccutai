Our Technology

MagicCutAI is powered by VEO3 technology, representing the latest breakthrough in AI-driven content generation. Our platform combines three revolutionary AI tools:

AI Video Generation: Create cinematic videos with perfect character consistency, natural dialogue, and professional cinematography. Our technology maintains character appearance and personality across multiple scenes, delivering Hollywood-quality results.

AI Image Creation: Generate stunning, high-resolution images up to 4K quality with style consistency and brand alignment. Perfect for marketing, social media, and creative projects with full commercial usage rights.

AI Logo Design: Design professional logos and complete brand identities with AI precision. Get scalable vector files, brand guidelines, and trademark-safe designs instantly.

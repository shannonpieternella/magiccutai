Data Deletion Instructions

At MagicCut AI, we respect your privacy and provide you with control over your personal data. If you would like to request the deletion of your data from our systems, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to info@magiccutai.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your full name
    • The email address associated with your account
    • Any additional information that can help us identify your account
  4. We will process your request and confirm the deletion within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information and profile data
  • Any content or media you have uploaded
  • Usage data and preferences associated with your account
  • Any other personal information we have collected
Please Note: Once your data is deleted, this action cannot be undone. You will lose access to your account and any associated content. We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this data deletion process, please contact us at:

Email: info@magiccutai.com

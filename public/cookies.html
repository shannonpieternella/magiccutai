Cookie Policy

Learn about how we use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners about user behavior and preferences.

This Cookie Policy explains how MagicCutAI ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Upsell Business Agency, uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Consent Required
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, authentication, and security Session/Persistent No (Strictly necessary)
Performance Cookies Collect information about how you use our website to improve performance Up to 2 years Yes
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings to personalize your experience Up to 1 year Yes
Analytics Cookies Help us understand user behavior and improve our services Up to 2 years Yes
Marketing Cookies Used to deliver personalized advertisements and track campaign effectiveness Up to 1 year Yes

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies: Keep you logged in to your account
  • Security cookies: Protect against cross-site request forgery and other security threats
  • Load balancing cookies: Ensure optimal website performance
  • Session cookies: Maintain your session state during your visit
  • Cookie consent cookies: Remember your cookie preferences

4. Performance and Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage, page views, and user behavior
  • Hotjar: Records user sessions to improve user experience
  • Performance monitoring: Monitors website speed and functionality
  • Error tracking: Helps us identify and fix technical issues

Analytics Data

All analytics data is collected in aggregate form and does not personally identify individual users. We use this data to improve our AI services, optimize performance, and enhance user experience.

5. Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Language preferences: Remember your selected language
  • Theme settings: Save your dark/light mode preference
  • User interface preferences: Remember your layout and display settings
  • Recently used features: Quick access to frequently used tools
  • Saved projects: Local storage of your work-in-progress content

6. Marketing and Advertising Cookies

With your consent, we use marketing cookies to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements on other websites
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ad repeatedly
  • Provide personalized content recommendations
  • Track conversions and attribute them to specific campaigns

Third-Party Advertising Partners

  • Google Ads: For search and display advertising
  • Facebook Pixel: For social media advertising
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: For professional network advertising
  • Twitter Pixel: For social media marketing

7. Cookie Management and Consent

You have several options for managing cookies:

Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about our cookie usage

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Important Note

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and services. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for basic website operation and security.
